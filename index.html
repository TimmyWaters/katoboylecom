ABOUT ME

My name is Katherine – you may call me Kat. I reside in Montgomery, Alabama. I currently work for a national pharmacy and retail chain. I am earning a Bachelors of Science Degree in Media Arts and Animation. I love animation in general because the human limitations do not apply. Anything you can imagine can be drawn and animated. Some animations are fantastical while others are more realistic or “human-like”. Some teach life lessons and some take you on incredible journeys (some combine both). I connect with both types of animations as the fantastical appeals to my adventurous/daydream imagination while the realistic animations appeal to the emphatic connection between the situations of the characters and real-life experiences. Alice in Wonderland is my favorite movie – both the original animation and the newest rendition created by Tim Burton.

I am a 2017 National Technical Honor Society Inductee at the Art Institute of Pittsburgh Online Division. I also am a member of the Online Volunteers Club. During my personal time, I read Dean R. Koontz and Tami Hoag books, watch CG movies and Anime, listen to music, make jewelry, take photographs, and, most importantly, spend time with my number one muse, my little impish daughter.

Katherine O'Boyle-Sheffield


HOBBIES:

  • Amateur Photographer/Videographer – I have a variety of subjects I like to shoot. My main snaps are of my daughter, clouds, water, shadows, and background textures. I am currently working with a local band, Abusements, to produce their first music video.
  • Drawing – I enjoy designing characters, their props, and environments. I usually draw anthropomorphic characters which have humorous traits and stories.
  • Writing – I write autobiographies and storylines for my own characters. I have written short stories, essays, a song, and poetry.
  • Reading – When I am feeling whimsical, I bring out my Jim Davis Garfield collection. When I wish to escape to an alternate realm, I pull out a suspense thriller, science fiction novel, or magickal laden tome.
  • Singing – Anything on the radio which appeals to my current mood and with my boyfriend as he plays acoustic.
  • Jewelry Design – Mainly costume jewelry – everything from actual costume designs for Halloween to beachy seashells to Victorian Gothic chokers.
  • Learning French as a second language.
  • Google Local Guide - Posting photos, critiques, and adding missing business listings.

CAREER GOALS:

  • Bachelors of Science Degree in Media Arts and Animation from the Art Institute of Pittsburgh Online Division: Projected Graduation Date February 2018
  • 3D and 2D Design, Photography, Editing, Production, Advertising, Marketing, Cinematography, Videography
  • Gain experience by working for a television station, design business, advertising company, and/or animation studio to produce advertisements, motion graphics, animations, commercials, television idents, 3D animations, and title sequences
  • Art Director
  • Producer

My ultimate dream goal is to create a 3D animation or live-action film in Anime style for IMAX theaters.
